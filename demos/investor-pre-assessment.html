Early Stage Investors

    Early Stage Investor Pre-assessment
    This pre-assessment will assist you in determining if you may qualify for ESIC tax incentives for the current financial year. You may potentially qualify for up to $200,000 in tax offsets and have your investment be CGT free for 10 years.
    If you purchase newly issued shares in a qualifying ESIC and hold them for 12 months, you will have a CGT exemption for up to 10 years when you sell your investment.
