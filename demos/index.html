Eligibility Basics

Starting up a business is a major undertaking, so its easy to assume that you are breaking new ground and destined to be eligible under these concessions, when in fact the measures do not allow for many early stage companies. 

To help make that process clearer we've developed online pre-assessments and put a lot of work into FAQ and other guides, though often companies and investors will ponder, am I in principal an eligible early stage company?

To over simplify, you will need to answer 2 of 3 questions in the affirmative; 1) is this an eligible early stage company? and a) do you command 100 points or more from the objective innovation test? or b) do you pass the principals based innovation test?

Please keep in mind that these are targeted measures that will be subject to review by the Australian Tax Office. The onus is upon the investor to prove that they were a qualifying investor, and the company allotting (issuing) the shares was an eligible early stage innovation company (ESIC).

Within the attached pages, you will find substantial references, listings and commentary regarding the requirements. Please do not hesitate to utilise them, contact us directly, or complete one or both of the online pre-assessments. We look-forward to listing your company (be that an investment or as your own early stage venture).
